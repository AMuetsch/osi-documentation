Data qualifier communicates the overall availability of the interface.

Values:

enumerator DATA_QUALIFIER_UNKNOWN¶

Unknown (must not be used in ground truth).

enumerator DATA_QUALIFIER_OTHER¶

Other (unspecified but known).

enumerator DATA_QUALIFIER_AVAILABLE¶

Data is available.

enumerator DATA_QUALIFIER_AVAILABLE_REDUCED¶

Reduced data is available.

enumerator DATA_QUALIFIER_NOT_AVAILABLE¶

Data is not available.

enumerator DATA_QUALIFIER_BLINDNESS¶

Sensor is blind.

enumerator DATA_QUALIFIER_TEMPORARY_AVAILABLE¶

Sensor temporary available.
